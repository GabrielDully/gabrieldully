Specialization Tracks at IME-USP

At IME-USP, Computer Science students can follow specialization tracks ("trilhas") that guide elective course choices and deepen knowledge in a specific area. These tracks help structure academic progression and may lead to official certification.

  • Software Systems: Focuses on building robust, scalable, and maintainable systems. Covers areas like software engineering, databases, distributed systems, and agile methodologies.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Prepares students to develop systems capable of cognitive tasks. Includes machine learning, robotics, knowledge-based systems, and AI theory.
  • Data Science: Emphasizes statistical analysis, machine learning, and large-scale data processing. Ideal for those who want to extract insights and build data-driven applications.
  • Theoretical Computer Science: Delves into algorithms, automata theory, optimization, and discrete mathematics. Suitable for students interested in computational theory and advanced problem solving.

Among the available specialization tracks at IME-USP, I chose to follow the Software Systems track.
